What Makes Old Tech Interesting?

Old technology shows how people solved problems before modern devices. The designs are often bulky, creative, and surprisingly durable.

  1. Reasons I collect old tech
    • It preserves technological history.
    • Many devices are repairable.
    • Vintage design is fascinating.
  2. Favorite types
    • Old computers
    • Vintage cameras
    • Retro game systems
